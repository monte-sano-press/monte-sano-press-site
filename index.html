About Monte Sano Press

An independent publishing company representing a select group of authors with distinct voices, audiences, and bodies of work.

Monte Sano Press publishes contemporary fiction with an emphasis on strong storytelling, clear positioning, and professional presentation.

The company represents authors whose work speaks to different readerships while maintaining a shared commitment to quality, substance, and memorable books.

Current authors include A.J. Beckett and James Tolliver.
